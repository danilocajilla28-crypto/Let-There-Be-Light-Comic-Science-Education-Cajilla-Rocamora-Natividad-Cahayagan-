Synopsis

Following the total exhaustion of Earth's resources, the ruthless CEO of Vance Energy Corp assassinates a coalition of clean-energy scientists and divides Eos City into a wealthy Utopia and a freezing, rationed slum. Sixteen-year-old Sirius — desperate to save his mother — infiltrates the elite Uptown sector and crosses paths with Ernest, leader of the underground resistance Project Prometheus. Operating from an abandoned nuclear cooling tower, the team launches a high-stakes mission to restore power — using the principles of nuclear fission to ignite a revolution and return the light to a city left in the dark.

The Science Behind the Story

Nuclear Energy — Key Concepts

⚛️

Nuclear Fission

When a heavy nucleus (like Uranium-235) splits, it releases enormous energy — embodied in Einstein's E = mc² — far exceeding any chemical reaction, enabling compact and long-lasting power generation.

🔵

Cherenkov Radiation

The iconic blue glow seen in nuclear reactors: produced when charged particles travel faster than light does in water — a real, beautiful phenomenon referenced throughout this comic.

⚖️

Criticality (K=1)

A reactor achieves criticality when exactly one neutron from each fission triggers another fission. Sirius's goal — balancing to K=1 — is the precise condition for a controlled, self-sustaining chain reaction.

🌿

Clean Energy

Nuclear power produces virtually zero greenhouse gas emissions during operation. Modern reactors are among the safest energy sources per unit of power generated — a vital tool in the clean energy transition.
